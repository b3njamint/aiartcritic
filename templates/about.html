Development Notes

Instructions

If you want to use an existing image:
Find an image of your choice (a famous painting, a flower, a meme, anything works!), then right click and copy the image address. If you copy the image and not the image address, it will not work. Paste the image address at the "Image URL" text input box.

If you want to use your own image:
Upload your image in the "Choose File" file input box. Although files up to 10 MB will be accepted, I recommend limiting file size to < 1 MB.

Click "Analyze," and twiddle your thumbs for a few moments as the algorithms process your request. Please note that analysis may take up to 30 seconds on a slow internet connection.
